A Bit About Me

I’m a Software Engineer based in Michigan, who loves clean, simple & unique designs. I also enjoy sightseeing and stargazing.

Savannah Sou

Since I was young, I’ve been passionate about video games and technology, even building my own computer from scratch. This passion led me to pursue computer science in college, where I worked multiple part-time jobs while being a full-time student to obtain my associate’s degree. Balancing work and school was exhausting, often leaving me drained, but my determination to build a future in tech kept me going. Though financial constraints prevented me from completing a bachelor’s, I found a cost-effective way to break into the field by attending a bootcamp. Seizing that opportunity, I became a Software Engineer and have enjoyed every moment of coding, blending my love for tech and creativity.

I’m now focused on refining my website project, GetFitWit, improving its functionality and design to enhance the user experience while continuing to grow as a developer.
